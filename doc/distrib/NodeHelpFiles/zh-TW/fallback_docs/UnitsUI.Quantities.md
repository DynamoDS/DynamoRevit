## 曆긷뀯蘊뉓쮭
`Quantities` provides a selectable quantity type (such as Length, Area, or Volume) and extracts the associated unit definitions. These quantities represent the type of measurement being described, not the actual unit value. For example, Length is the quantity, while Feet or Meters are units that belong to that quantity.
